Overview

The iPhone 12 features a stunning 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, powerful A14 Bionic chip, and an advanced dual-camera system. With 5G capability and MagSafe technology, it's designed to elevate your experience to the next level.

  • Fully unlocked and compatible with any carrier of choice
  • 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display with 2532x1170 resolution
  • A14 Bionic chip with next-generation Neural Engine
  • Advanced dual-camera system with 12MP Ultra Wide and Wide cameras
  • Ceramic Shield with 4x better drop performance
